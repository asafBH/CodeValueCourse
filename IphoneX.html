Welcome to the iPhone X Page

Description

The iPhone X (Roman numeral "X" pronounced "ten") is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It was the eleventh generation of the iPhone, announced on September 12, 2017, alongside the iPhone 8 and iPhone 8 Plus at the Steve Jobs Theater in the Apple Park campus. Released on November 3, 2017, it marked the iPhone series' tenth anniversary and showcased what Apple considers technology of the future.

Featuring OLED screen technology for the first time, the iPhone X has a glass and stainless-steel form factor, wireless charging, and a new "bezel-less" design. Notably, it introduces Face ID, a new authentication method utilizing advanced facial recognition technology.
